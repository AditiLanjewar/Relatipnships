Relationships

asaan hoti hain?



Jitna sochne aur dekhne

mein lagta hai asaan hai kisi

ke saath rehna ek relationship

mein, utna hota nahi hai...



Wajah log hi hote hain, do

alag log hote hain, dono alag

personality, dono ki soch alag,

lifestyle alag, har cheez alag.



Cheezein match hote hote bhi

kayi baar ek point aisa ata hai

jab dono ka opinion badal jata

hai aur waha conflicts start ho

jate hain.



Log hote imperfect hi hain,

wo galtiya bhi karte hain,

galtiya dohrate bhi hain,

unme koi na koi kami

bhi hoti hi hai.



Agar aapke mann mein feelings

hain unke liye to aap unhein wo

jaise hain waise accept karte hain.



Problems ayengi, lekin mattter

ye karta hai ki aap kis ke saath

un problems ko deal karna chahte ho.



Give up karne wali cheez tab

tak nahi aani chahiye jab tak,

aapka rishta puri tarah se

toxic na ho jaye.



In rishto mein kayi baar

dil bhi tut ta hai, kayi baar

bharosa bhi tut ta hai, kayi

baar chot bhi lagti hai.



Isliye aisa kuch bhi nahi hai

ki koi fairy tale jaisa kuch hoga.

Practically, relationships are hard.



Lekin, sab logo par depend

karta hai, kyunki log hi cheezo

ko complicated banate hain.



Aur wahi smooth.

To agar ho kisi ke saath ya aane

ki sooch rahe ho relationship mein.



Prepare raho best aur worst

experience karne ke liye.
